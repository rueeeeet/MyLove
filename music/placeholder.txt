BACKGROUND MUSIC GUIDE
======================

Add your romantic song here!

Required File:
-------------
1. love-song.mp3 - Your chosen background music

Music Specifications:
--------------------
- Format: MP3 (recommended) or OGG
- Bitrate: 128kbps or 192kbps
- Keep file size under 10MB
- Choose a soft, romantic instrumental or love song

Song Suggestions:
----------------
- "Can't Help Falling in Love" - Elvis Presley
- "Perfect" - Ed Sheeran (Instrumental)
- "A Thousand Years" - Christina Perri
- "All of Me" - John Legend (Instrumental)
- Any song that's meaningful to your relationship!

Where to Get Music:
------------------
- Use royalty-free music from YouTube Audio Library
- Convert your favorite songs (for personal use only)
- Use instrumental versions to avoid copyright issues

Note:
-----
Make sure the filename is exactly: love-song.mp3
Or update the filename in index.html if you use a different name.
