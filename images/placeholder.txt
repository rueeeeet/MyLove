PLACEHOLDER IMAGES GUIDE
========================

Replace these placeholder instructions with your actual photos!

Required Images:
---------------
1. first-meet.jpg - Photo from when you first met
2. first-date.jpg - Photo from your first date
3. special-moment.jpg - Any special moment together
4. recent.jpg - A recent photo of you both
5. adventure-poster.jpg - Thumbnail for video (optional)

Image Specifications:
--------------------
- Format: JPG, PNG, or WEBP
- Recommended size: 800x600 pixels or larger
- Keep file sizes under 2MB for faster loading

Quick Tip:
----------
You can use any photo editing app to resize your images before uploading.
Make sure the filenames match exactly what's in the HTML!

Temporary Solution:
------------------
Until you add your photos, the site will show broken image icons.
To create quick placeholders, you can use:
- https://via.placeholder.com/800x600
- Or use any photo editing software to create colored rectangles with text
