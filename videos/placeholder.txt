VIDEO FILES GUIDE
=================

Add your video files here!

Required Videos:
---------------
1. adventure.mp4 - A video of your adventures together

Video Specifications:
--------------------
- Format: MP4 (H.264 codec recommended)
- Recommended size: 1280x720 (720p) or 1920x1080 (1080p)
- Keep file sizes reasonable (under 50MB for web)
- Duration: 30 seconds to 2 minutes works best

Tips:
-----
- Compress videos using tools like HandBrake or online compressors
- Test the video plays in your browser before deploying
- You can use your phone's video editor to trim and compress

Optional:
---------
Create a thumbnail image (adventure-poster.jpg) for the video
to show before it plays!
